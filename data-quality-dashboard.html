Data Quality Dashboard

Get visibility into causes & effects of data quality issues & improve accountability.

Background/ Challenge/ Brief

Data defects have a direct impact on the processes firms use to support customer interactions. A problem many data quality improvement processes suffer from is lack of visibility into causes and effects of data quality issues. The other problem is holding specific organisations accountable for improving overall data quality.

The initial ideas and approach were centred around an organisational centric view-“organisation having departments doing work to solve customer problems”. However, since the exercise was about impacts on customer experience, we proposed a design that focuses on the customer journey.

This changed the vantage point to “customers in their journeys being helped by parts of an organisation”. Customer experience data at various touch points was available, and we decided to visualise these as part of the customer journey, rather than in isolation for every touchpoint. We took inspiration from the idea of a service blueprint for the final visualisation.

Stages of the customer journey as per the LBGUPS (Learn, Buy, Get, Use, Pay, Service) model are visualised against the various channels over which the service is delivered. Each touchpoint highlighted in red has reported CX issues. Data defect rates for these channels is also visualised.

One glance to convey whether things are on track. A birds eye view of where the organisation is on the various CX improvement projects. Each item can be drilled down to see a similar view at a deeper level in the organisation. These have traceability with the customer journey, touchpoint and system details shown above.

Clicking on any touchpoint in the customer journey map would bring the user to a view with details about CX issues with their impacts and causes, data defect rates for various systems responsible for that touchpoint. Further drilling down can be done to associate data objects with people responsible and impacted organisations.

Primary Users
Data Steward

Key Goals Addressed
Reduce the impacts of defective data on our business operations and customer experiences by improving data quality.
